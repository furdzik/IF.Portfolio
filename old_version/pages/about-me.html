I Introduce myself

When I have 13, together with my friend, we made our first site. It come awful, but from that moment, I got charmed by this world of creating. I started with blog's skins. It was neither pretty nor good - but I enjoyed it.
I found my first job accidentally. On my first day in, I’ve learned that I’ve no clue on how to create websites and I had to learn everything from the scratch. It wasn't that hard and after a few days I made my first real site. I don’t remember exactly when, but making sites started to passionate me, and was really joyful.

Now - after 6 years - I still love what I do, and it still makes me happy.

Of course I haven't finished my education yet. Every day I discover new things and someday I would like to be an authority in the matter. My idols are Eric Meyer, Jeffrey Zeldman, Ethan Marcotte. I admire them because they create new solutions to creating websites.

Also someday I would like to work for W3C and contribute to the development of new technologies for creating sites.
